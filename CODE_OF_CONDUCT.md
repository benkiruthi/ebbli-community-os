# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Ebbli Community OS a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, socioeconomic status, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:

- showing empathy and respect;
- welcoming different perspectives and lived experiences;
- giving constructive feedback;
- accepting responsibility and correcting mistakes;
- prioritizing the wellbeing of the community.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory remarks;
- sexualized language or unwanted attention;
- publishing another person's private information;
- deliberate intimidation or disruption;
- abuse of project systems, community members, or moderators.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and participation that violate this Code of Conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. Do not disclose sensitive information publicly. Reports will be reviewed as promptly and confidentially as practical.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces and when someone officially represents the project in public spaces.
